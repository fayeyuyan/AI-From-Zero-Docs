# Intro to AI
A - Artificial

I - Intelligence, which can perform cognitive tasks such as thinking and decision-making.

    eg, UKG - providing faster thinking and informed business decisions.
  
      Jiva - providing sustainable & reliable farming solution.

# How AI works
Machine learning(ML) - A subset of AI focused on analyzing data to make decisions or predictions efficiently.

Training set - A collection of data used to teach AI

Training data quality and relevance are critical for effective ML programs; data bias is a fundamental issue that can cause inaccurate AI outputs.
